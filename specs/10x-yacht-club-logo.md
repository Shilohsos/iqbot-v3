### FORMAT
- 1920 × 1080 landscape
- Dark theme, PNG
- 10x Bot brand system (see corner brackets)

### BRAND PALETTE
| Role | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Primary | `#A356FF` | Corner brackets, glow effects |
| Accent | `#C750FF` | Tagline, subtle highlights |
| Background | `#190B28` | Full canvas fill |
| Gold | `#D4AF37` or `#F0C060` | Anchor/icon, main title |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Title text |

### LAYOUT (top to bottom)

1. **Corner brackets — top**
   - Top-left: thin line extending right + thin line extending down, `#A356FF`
   - Top-right: thin line extending left + thin line extending down, `#A356FF`
   - Lines: 2-3px thick, ~80px arm length

2. **Center icon** (vertical center ~40% from top)
   - Minimalist gold anchor OR abstract geometric yacht silhouette
   - Rendered in gold (`#D4AF37`) with subtle purple glow (`#A356FF` at 40% opacity behind)
   - NOT: cartoon, 3D, realistic boat, ocean water, clipart
   - Clean, geometric, premium — think luxury marina logo
   - Icon size: ~300-400px wide

3. **Title**
   - "10x YACHT CLUB" directly below icon
   - Uppercase, sans-serif (Montserrat or similar)
   - Large: ~72-96pt
   - Color: white with optional gold gradient overlay
   - Letter spacing: 4-6px for premium feel

4. **Tagline**
   - "ELITE TRADING CIRCLE" below title
   - Smaller: ~28-36pt
   - Color: `#C750FF`
   - Uppercase, letter spacing: 8-12px

5. **Watermark**
   - "by 10x AI" in small text, bottom center
   - Color: `#A356FF` at 30% opacity
   - ~14pt, subtle

6. **Corner brackets — bottom**
   - Bottom-left: thin line extending right + thin line extending up, `#A356FF`
   - Bottom-right: thin line extending left + thin line extending up, `#A356FF`
   - Mirrored from top brackets

### VIBE
Exclusive private marina. Members-only yacht club. Dark luxury — not loud, not flashy. Premium quiet. The kind of place where money moves silently.

### NO
- Boats with sails
- Cartoon anchors
- Ocean water, waves, beaches
- 3D renders
- Clipart
- Gradients that look like Instagram filters
- Overly bright or neon colors

Brand system: 10x AI corner bracket style (same as other 10x Bot specs at /var/www/10xbot/specs/)
